Survey 1 : for SL undergradute students
  • This survey is conducted by the AI4COVID project group of the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya. The purpose of this survey is to collect basic socio-economic details specially households information on demographic characteristics, education and health of undergraduates during the pandemic period.

    The main objective of this project is to propose Artificial Intelligence Framework based strategies to mitigate the negative impacts of pandemic.

    Please note that the confidentiality of data is guaranteed.

    For more details about the project refer to our website https://covid.eng.pdn.ac.lk/
